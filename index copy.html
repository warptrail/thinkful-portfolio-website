History

I first got into web development when I launched a Wordpress site two years ago. Using plugins like Elementor gave me a glimpse at how to customize a layout. But it felt too cookie-cutter and static. I want to create a web page that does something and gives the user a more imersed experience. So that's why I'm joining Thinkful. To get to that next level.

I have been tinkering with web development for over a year now. The self-taught route has included probably over a hundred hours of Youtube, SkillShare, Udemy and Zenva tutorials. I am excited to accelerate my learning process by going 100% all into this path!

I'm the type of person who really appreciates using a well designed digital product. All this life-changing technology all around us is all so relatively new. The internet has only been around 30 years or so and look how much it's changed things. Imagine what the world will be like when the internet has been around for 300 years?
